ST.Francis School, Indirapuram.

St. Francis School, Indirapuram, is a harbinger in the field of education in NCR region of Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh. It is a Christian educational institution established in 2004, owned and run by the Franciscan Brothers (Congregation of the Missionary Brothers of Saint Francis of Assisi - CMSF). The It was the ardent love of Christ that led the Brothers to cater to the St. need of the urban population of Indirapuram for an intellectually and morally sound quality education. As a Christian Minority co-educational institution, it is open to the children of all communities regardless of colour, caste and creed. It follows the syllabus of the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), Delhi and the medium of instruction TI is English. The school is located on a sprawling campus with standard sports facilities of Swimming Pool, Badminton, Table Tennis, Tennis courts, state-of-art auditorium, AV (Audio Visual) room, indoor stadium etc. From a humble genesis the school has grown in nature and stature. Today we have more than 3000 students under our tutelage along with 130 teachers and 56 Administrative and auxiliary staff.
